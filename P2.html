ACTION DESCRIPTION IMAGE
Two objects of the Particle class are created which are used to simulate the collision mechanism.
Translation /
Rotation 		The translation and rotation of the objects is simlar to the previous program. Translation for both objects occurs when keys 'k' and 'j' are pressed. Similarly, rotation occurs when either of 'e' or 'r' are pressed.
Methods Involved: translateVelocity() ,
rotateVelocity() , rotate() , translate() ,
rebound()
Collision When the two objects collide, they rebound of each other, and the resulatant velocity is calculated based on the coefficient of restitution of the objects. For easy simulation, the collision is assumed to be elastic.
